Welcome to TripSync

Connection The application tries to connect nearest device on startup, but in case you face any difficulties, you can simply click on the "Connect" to pair the device. The indicator on right states the connection status. For multiple panels, selection dropdown will be visibel. Color Pallete The color pallete offers 16 million colors to choose from, with an additional choice of whiteness and some predefined colors. Patterns The pattern selector slide lets you select between a variety of modes, each of which can be marked as favourite based on your preference. Scenes Create your personalized scenes that cater your mood the most using 'Create a scene'. Created scenes can be accessed in the 'Scenes' tab. All set!
